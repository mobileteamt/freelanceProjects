About Me

Deane's Portfolio

DEANE SEQUEIRA (D-ANN) Rapper-Singer-Songwriter-Composer A Mumbai based Indie female Rapper-Songwriter- Recording Artist in the Indian Music-Film & Advertising Industry. A Rockschool Music - London Trained & Certified Vocalist & Western India Topper in the vocal category begun her professional Music career when she was 15 years, during her school days. Deane is a renowned rapper and is credited for her Vocal contributions & Lyrics in several Bollywood films, TVC Jingles, OTT, Independent and Bollywood songs in big banner films......BEZUBAAN - ABCD (Anybody Can Dance), Student of the Year 2, Welcome Back, Nikamma, Street Dancer, SuperGirl, Luv u Soniyo, Purani Jeans, Holiday, Welcome to Karachi, Sabse Bhala Rupaiya-SaReGaMa Music, Jashn-e-Hungama, Kya Thi Meri Galti, Netflix- BassBaby, Duniya Hamein Yaad Karein, Timeflies, Only Mine, Nobody Cares, Random. Deane has extensively worked in the media & advertisement industry and lent her voice for many Jingles........ Hyundai, Myntra, JEEP , VIVO, Britannia WinKin Cow, Ponds, P&G Whispers India, Sugar Cosmetics, Campco Chocolates, Unilever SuperGirl, MacDonald, Eva Deo, Mobiistar, Roposo, OneFitPlus, Nair Cosmetics, Sporto Macho, Gillette, Free FireMax, Xpress Money Dubai, Priya Gold, Gagan, Mochi, Irus Idee, RoyalStag-Delhi Capitals IPL song & Many More......... Deane has performed Live at Asia's biggest Music festivals- Awestrung Reloded Music Festival. 50 Artist - 1 Marathon The NH7 Weekender Pune with The Ram Sampath Experience Band, the Priyanka Chopra-Facebook Social for Good initiative campaign and Bollywood Music Project-BMP 2019 Music Festival. As a collaborative rapper she has worked & performed live with inspiring Singers & Musicians. Deane has also extensively worked with most eminent Music industry producers & directors......Meet Brothers, Vishal-Shekhar, Sachin-Jigar, Pritam, Sajid-Wajid, Vipin Patwa, Aditya Dev, Javed-Mohsin, Saurabh-Durgesh, Arya Acharya and Many More...... A Ex- SONY India Music artist is currently signed with Hungama-Artist Aloud and exclusively managed by "5th String Inc".
